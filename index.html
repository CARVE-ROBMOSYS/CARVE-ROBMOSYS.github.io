--- layout: page title: CARVE subtitle: ComposAble Robot behaViors with vErification use-site-title: true --- CARVE is an Integrated Tecnical Partner (ITP) of the European Union project RobMosys. With the CARVE project, the consortium proposes Behavior Trees (BTs) for modelling composable and reusable behaviors and proposes the use of formal methods for increasing confidence in their correct execution. The tools developed in CARVE are validated on real-world scenario using the R1 humanoid robot. The integration of the YARP middleware (used on the R1 robot) and the SmartSoft toolkit helps disseminating the RobMoSys approach to the research community.
The CARVE project has received funding from the European Union's Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under grant agreement No 732410, in the form of financial support to third parties of the RobMoSys project.
